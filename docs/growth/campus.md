| URL | Role |
|-----|------|

## Tone rules (non-negotiable)

- Clear, direct, respectful of a smart 18-year-old
- No slang-chasing, no “fellow kids,” no outrage bait
- Broadsheet identity stays; grades are the hook

## Share loops

1. Student copies pack on `/students/` → Discord / iMessage  
2. Single explainer “Copy for Discord / chat”  
3. OG card unfurl on Bluesky / X / iMessage  
4. Quiz streak → free register  

## Ops

- Link `/students/` from class visits, club nights, orientation tables  
- Peg explainers to midterm weeks (lean + first-vote)  
- Never invent campus programs or stats in copy
